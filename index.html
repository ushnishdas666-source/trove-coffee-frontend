A Treasure in Kolkata

Trove Coffee & More was born from a simple love for exceptional coffee and heartfelt conversations. Nestled in the vibrant heart of Kolkata, we've created a sanctuary where every cup tells a story and every guest feels like family. Our beans are sourced ethically, roasted locally, and brewed with passion by our expert baristas.

We're more than just a coffee shop; we're a community hub, a quiet place to work, and a warm spot to unwind. Welcome to your treasure trove of flavours and comfort.
